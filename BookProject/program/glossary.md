# Master Glossary

Single source of truth for terminology across both products. Each entry has two definitions:

- **Plain English** — used in Product A (general public, Grade 10).
- **Technical** — used in Product B (analyst series, university level).

Keep entries alphabetical. Add an entry the first time a term is used in either product.

---

## Entry template

Copy the block below for each new term.

```markdown
### Term name

- **Plain English (A):** One or two sentences. No jargon. A reader with no policy background should understand it.
- **Technical (B):** Full definition with statutory basis, governing legislation, and any contested usage. May reference other glossary terms.
- **First appears in:** A Ch.X / B Vol.Y Ch.Z
- **Related terms:** [comma-separated list]
- **Sources:** [authoritative reference, e.g., Constitution s.X, MFMA s.Y, StatsSA definition]
- **Notes:** Any cross-product translation issues, or terms the editor flagged as risky.
```
