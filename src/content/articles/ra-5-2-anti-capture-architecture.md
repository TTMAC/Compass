## What Capture Actually Means

## Vector 1: Political Capture

<!-- Section: Patronage, cadre deployment, tariff suppression for electoral gain. Structural defences: protected appointments, independent boards. -->

## Vector 2: Commercial Capture

<!-- Section: Procurement fraud, contractor collusion, inflated contracts. Structural defences: e-procurement, open contracting, automatic price benchmarking. -->

## Vector 3: Labour Capture

<!-- Section: Resistance to performance management, bloated headcount, union veto over operational decisions. Structural defences: outcome-based contracts, right-sizing mechanisms. -->

## Vector 4: Regulatory Capture

<!-- Section: Weak enforcement, revolving doors, regulatory discretion. Structural defences: automatic triggers, separation of functions, cooling-off periods. -->

## Vector 5: Information Capture

<!-- Section: Management filtering data to oversight bodies, delayed reporting, selective disclosure. Structural defences: automated data pipelines, public dashboards, whistleblower protection. -->
